Andrew Ross Sorkin, author of Too Big to Fail

November 2015

New York Times colummist and author, Andrew Rose Sorkin has been described as "the most famous financial of his generation." His book,Too Big To Fail, was the first behind-the scenece account of the financial crisis that led to our recession.

The Economist, The Financial Times, and Bussiness Week all named Too Big to Fail one of the best books of the years. The book was published by Viking October 20,2009. The book was adapted as a movie by HBO Films and premiered on HBO on May 23,2011.
